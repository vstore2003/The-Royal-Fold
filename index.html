About The Royal Fold

Founded by passionate turban experts, The Royal Fold brings the ancient art of turban tying to the modern world with precision and elegance.

Our team of skilled professionals has mastered various traditional and contemporary styles, ensuring you look your best for any occasion.

Serving the Greater Toronto Area with locations in Brampton and Mississauga, we're dedicated to preserving this cultural tradition while adapting to modern fashion trends.
